Respected Deans/Faculty Members,
Assalam O Alaikum
Reference: DCTM No. 28/2018 dated 28 February 2018.

The Learning Management System(LMS) is ready by now for 1st LMS Feedback from 12 Mar to 25 Mar 2018 for students and all of the activities like Creation of Courses, Enrollments of Faculty and Students, Creation of accounts for new students and faculty members have been completed for Spring Semester 2018 on Learning Management System (LMS).

Faculty members and Students are requested to check:
  1. The courses being taught by the respective faculty members
  2. The total number of students are enrolled for the specific course
  3. The classes/courses are scheduled according to the latest Approved Time Table
Note : New faculty members and students are also requested to get the login credentials (username/password) for LMS from Help Desk / QA Office.
Feel free to contact QA Office (53035) or email to LMS Coordinator (kashifkhan@pnec.nust.edu.pk) in case of any ambiguity/discrepancy.

Best Regards,
